How to Use

1

Apply

Apply a small amount of Chebe Oil to your palms and rub together.

2

Massage

Gently massage the oil into your scalp and through your hair.

3

Leave In

Leave the oil in your hair for at least 30 minutes or overnight.

4

Rinse

Rinse thoroughly and style as usual. Use 2-3 times per week.
